Salon: Speaking Of Abstraction III
Tuesday 27 November, 6.30—8pm
£4 / £2 conc. / Free to ESP members

This interdisciplinary talk will explore sensory perception, sound and physical space with presentations by Dr Massimiliano Di Luca, Lecturer in Computational Neuroscience at Birmingham University and Jamie Bullock co-founder of Integra Lab, a music technology research centre based at the Birmingham Conservatoire.

and

A Vorratskammer (Pantry) Lunch
Organised with Kathrin Böhm / myvillages.org
Friday 30 November, 11–2pm

In a change to our monthly Lunch Club, in addition to welcoming you to join us for lunch there will be a special artists-travelling-larder to share. Artist Kathrin Böhm and and the Eastside Projects team will be collecting food for this special meal along their journeys between now and then. There will be also be an opportunity to see ‘The Vorratskammer’ book and edition produced by myvillages.org, and a documentary film about the project will be screened before and after the meal.

and

Artbus
Saturday 1 December, 12—6pm

We are delighted that the Art Bus will be stopping at Eastside Projects again this winter. The Art Bus is free and offers a great opportunity to see spaces and exhibitions across the city as we, mac Birmingham, Ikon Gallery, RBSA, Barber Institute and Birmingham Museum and Gallery open our doors. See our website to download the timetable.
