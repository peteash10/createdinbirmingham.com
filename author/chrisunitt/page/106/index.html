Lewes Herriot

December 23, 2009 by Chris Unitt

Lewes Herriot is a self-taught illustrator/artist who’s worked for Johnny Foreigner, Charlotte Hatherley, Tubelord, Free Moral Agents, NME, Fly Magazine, Fused Magazine, Topman Design, Ikon Gallery (Birmingham) and This Is Tomorrow. Busy chap. He’s got a MySpace page, a blog at The Dark Inventory and a Flickr account with plenty of work on show.
