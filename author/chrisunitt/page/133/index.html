Katarina Zdjelar – The Perfect Sound

December 8, 2008 by Chris Unitt

At VIVID until 20 December (but only Wed-Sat), The Perfect Sound is a ‘single channel video work’ which looks at the idea of creating a ‘perfect sound’ within the English language. Katarina Zdjelar has been in residence at VIVID for the past couple of months and this is her first solo exhibition here.
