If you’re looking for Halloween-y things to do around Birmingham then here are a few ideas:

28 October 2009

  • Filmrats Halloween special – a multimedia evening of short films and live music. Plus fancy dress

30 October 2009

  • Ghost walk at Highbury Hall. £9.95 and you get a jacket potato – can’t say fairer than that.
  • An American Werewolf in London at The Electric (and again on 31 Oct)

31 October 2009

  • The Phantom of the Opera – 1925 silent film at the Town Hall, with prizes for the best Halloween costume.
  • Cherry Pop Peep Show presents The Bare Witch Project. Burlesque, music and magic upstairs at The Victoria.

2 November 2009

  • Halloween Monster Movie Challenge 2009. Sound Bar will host the screening of the films submitted for the challenge

I’ve been cobbling this list together over the past few days – further suggestions would be welcome in the comments…
