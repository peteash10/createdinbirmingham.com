]]> http://www.createdinbirmingham.com/2009/02/13/birmingham-twestival/feed/ 2 Katie Parry’s Twitter-verse http://www.createdinbirmingham.com/2009/01/31/katie-parrys-twitter-verse/ http://www.createdinbirmingham.com/2009/01/31/katie-parrys-twitter-verse/#comments Sat, 31 Jan 2009 23:53:12 +0000 http://www.createdinbirmingham.com/?p=2930 [Read more...]]]> This week Wendy Cope, one of the favourites to become the new poet laureate said that you can’t write poetry to order. Channel 4 set a challenge to prove her wrong: Write a poem ‘to lift the spirits of the nation’ in the format of a 140 character Twitter tweet.

Katie Parry aka @supercoolkp, a designer with supercool, did just that and managed to get her tweet read on air on the evening news.

Katie said:

I can’t begin to tell you how chuffed I was – I watch Channel 4 News every weeknight so to see my (Twitter) name on the screen was brilliant.

And it’s another small yet powerful example of the amazing connective potential of Twitter. How else would a graphic designer in Brum get their poem recited on the national news?

The segment is available to watch on YouTube and the best of the rest are on Channel 4 online.
