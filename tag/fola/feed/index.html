At 8pm on Friday 24 October, the Songwriter’s Cafe is making a return, featuring:

  • Friends of The Stars
  • Leo Altarelli
  • Abie Budgen
  • Fola

It’ll be at The (ex-Orange) Studio in the city centre and will be hosted by Paul Murphy & Gary O’Dea.
