Provide is an independent men’s clothing and lifestyle shop based out of The Custard Factory. Supporting quality focused brands and local creatives, Provide has also recently released a publication of a limited print newspaper. This crisp little number has a collection of articles and photographs with a focus on Digbeth and the Birmingham cityscape.

They’ll be a second edition of the newspaper and Provide is on the look out for future contributors, so give them a shout if you want to provide (see what I did there?) something for the next round.

Pick up the paper from the Provide shop.
