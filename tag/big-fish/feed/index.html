Seems the free tickets to see Amelie were popular. So much so that they’ve all gone now. Sorry if you missed out but hark, there is good news!

  • 4.30pm – Big Fish

Incidentally, thanks to Jez Turner who suggested Twenty Four Seven when I was asking around for suggestions for feelgood films.
