There’s a Cure Leukaemia art auction being held at Wragge & Co’s offices (on Colmore Row) on Thurs 20 May. RSVP here.

Here’s the bluffer’s guide:

  • Fifth Art Auction set to raise £20,000 for Centre of Clinical Haematology at University Hospital Birmingham
  • Over £75,000 raised to date at annual event
  • Hosted by David Bintley, Director of Birmingham Royal Ballet
  • Auctioneer Jeremy Thornton, Managing Director of Biddle and Webb
  • Live Auction, Silent Auction and Twitter Auction
  • 30+ lots donated by West Midlands and UK based contemporary artists, photographers and designer-makers

It’s all in a very good cause and there’s some great art up for grabs too – check out the artists/lots.
