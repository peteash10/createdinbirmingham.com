university http://www.createdinbirmingham.com Fri, 17 Aug 2018 17:05:12 +0000 en-US hourly 1 http://www.createdinbirmingham.com/wp-content/uploads/cropped-CiB-Google-copy-32x32.jpg university http://www.createdinbirmingham.com 32 32 New Designers 2013 http://www.createdinbirmingham.com/2013/06/25/new-designers-2013/ Tue, 25 Jun 2013 13:31:52 +0000 http://www.createdinbirmingham.com/?p=15430 [Read more...]]]> New Designers 2013 kicks off tomorrow, 26th June, down in London at the Business Design Centre. New Designers aims to bring people face to face with the next generation of design leaders and provides a fantastic platform for thousands of graduates to launch their career.

There are many graduates from Birmingham City University and Birmingham Institute of Art and Design heading down to London to exhibit during the shows in a variety of areas.

There are students exhibiting their textiles work, students from the BA Hons Illustration and BA Hons Graphic Communication degrees will be showcasing in the Visual Communication zone and Interior Design students will feature in the second part of the show in the Spatial Design zone. Product & Furniture Design students and Fashion Design with Design for Performance students from Birmingham City University can be found during the second part of the show also!

Tickets are available online so if you’re around head over and support some of the new generation of Birmingham based designers.
