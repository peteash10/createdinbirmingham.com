We’ve just finished an animated trailer for the new Sega game Hell Yeah!  A completely Birmingham based project between ourselves writing, directing and art directing (and partly illustrating), Stevie Copter from Newtasty helping with the illustration and Character Shop doing the animation itself. The game isn’t released yet, but the trailer went out yesterday and its doing pretty well.

The other thing is a feature on a photographic book i’m working on over at CFYE in Amsterdam.
