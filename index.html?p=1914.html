Mecanoo win the contract to design Birmingham Library

August 5, 2008 by Chris Unitt

I have no real intention of following the interminable shenanigans surrounding the new library.Â  However, I reckon it’s worth announcing that the contract to design the new building has been given to Mecanoo (whose buildings had better be more attractive and useable than their website).

The new library, which at the rate things seem to be going won’t be built in any of our lifetimes (although 2013 has been mooted), is to be built in Centenary Square and will incorporate The REP, who have been quick to report on the appointment.

There’s more info from The Birmingham Post and Property Week.
